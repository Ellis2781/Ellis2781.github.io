Who we are

Artistic Coverings Inc is a Central Florida, Orlando area based countertop company. Artistic Coverings Inc provides our Central Florida customers with Granite, Quartz like Cambria and SileStone, other surfaces like Corian, Avonite, Stainless Steel, and many more countertop surfaces. For unique services we specialize in highly technical and creative custom Solid Surface projects, a service that sets us apart in the Orlando area. In addition to countertops we do bathroom partitions, custom bath-tub surrounds, custom shower bases and walls. We are located in the City of Longwood, just north of Orlando, Florida.
